About Us

Ethiopia's Finest Strawberry Farm, Powered by Precision Agriculture

Established on the slopes of Mount Entoto at 2,800 m above sea level, ENTOTO Riverside Farm harnesses the unique highland microclimate — cool nights, intense equatorial sun — to produce strawberries of exceptional sweetness and firm texture.

🏔️

Highland Advantage

Cool nights at 2,800 m slow sugar metabolism, building deeper flavour and longer shelf life than lowland fruit.

💧

Precision Drip Irrigation

Three independently controlled valve zones deliver water directly to the root zone, reducing waste and disease pressure.

🤖

AI Disease Detection

Gemini-powered image analysis spots botrytis, powdery mildew and nutrient deficiencies before they spread.

📦

Full Traceability

Every carton carries a batch number linking it to the exact bed, harvest date, and farmer who picked it.

How Every Berry Gets to You

🌱
1
Planting
Disease-free runners sourced from certified nurseries in Kenya and Ethiopia, planted in black plastic-mulched beds.
💧
2
Fertigation
Nutrients delivered directly to roots via drip irrigation — NPK, calcium nitrate, potassium sulfate on a precision schedule.
🔍
3
Monitoring
Daily field walks by trained supervisors, backed by AI image analysis for early disease detection and yield forecasting.
🍓
4
Harvest
Hand-picked at optimal ripeness each morning. Immediately weighed, labelled by bed ID, and moved to the pack house.
📦

Grading & Packing
Sorted into Grade A and B. Packed in 250 g, 500 g, 1 kg, and bulk cartons with full batch traceability QR codes.

Dispatch
Delivered same-day or next-morning to hotels, supermarkets, restaurants, and export partners across Addis Ababa.
Smart Farming

Technology Rooted in the Soil

We combine traditional Ethiopian agricultural wisdom with cutting-edge precision farming tools to grow better fruit, waste less, and protect our land.

🤖
Gemini AI Disease Detection
Supervisors photograph suspicious plants. Our Gemini-powered model classifies botrytis, powdery mildew, leaf spot and root rot with >85% confidence — often before visible symptoms escalate.
Powered by Google Gemini
📊
Live Harvest Dashboard
Real-time yield data per bed, per valve zone, and per variety. Managers see daily harvest trends, grade ratios, and packing efficiency at a glance from any device.
