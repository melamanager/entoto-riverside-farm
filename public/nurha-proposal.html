02 · The Challenge

What Manual Farm Management Costs You

Strawberry cultivation in Ethiopia is growing rapidly, but most farms — including many at your scale — still rely on paper logs, phone calls, and memory. This creates avoidable losses every season.

🔴 Disease Spreads Undetected

Without a structured reporting system, supervisors have no fast, documented way to flag diseases to management. By the time an outbreak is noticed, it has already spread to neighbouring beds.

🔴 No Harvest Visibility

Managers cannot see real-time yield data by bed or variety. Decisions on packaging, sales, and jam production are made on guesswork rather than actual harvest records.

🔴 Irrigation Guesswork

Valve schedules are managed informally. Over- or under-watering goes untracked until plant health deteriorates, by which point the damage is already done.

🔴 No Accountability Trail

When a crop fails, there is no record of who applied treatment, when, or whether they followed protocol. This makes it impossible to learn from losses or hold teams accountable.
